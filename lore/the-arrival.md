# The Arrival

**Classification:** Catastrophic event, pre-colonial  
**Status:** Effects ongoing; origin unresolved  
**Primary evidence:** Debris field analysis, Precursor ruin sites, biological survey  

---

## The Event

Several thousand years before human landing, an asteroid made impact with The Eye. The impact site is partially identifiable in the debris field region northeast of Firstfall.

By conventional metrics, the impact was not catastrophic. The object was not large enough to trigger mass extinction through kinetic energy, ejecta, or atmospheric disruption. Standard planetary impact models predict regional damage, not civilization-ending consequences.

The Precursor civilization ended anyway.

## The Rewriting Agent

The debris field carries coherence signatures that do not match The Eye's native field. They are older, different in frequency, and do not originate from this planet or this star system. The asteroid did not simply arrive — it carried something.

The working hypothesis developed through ruin analysis, biological survey, and coherence field study is as follows:

The impactor delivered a **rewriting agent** — not a biological organism, not a conventional pathogen, but a field-based entity that operates at the level of the coherence field itself. Its primary mechanism of action was the **field locus**: the coherence-stored component of Precursor genetics. Rather than destroying the Precursors through physical force, the Arrival altered their field loci directly — rewriting their biology at the level that existed outside their physical molecular structure.

The effect was not uniform. Some Precursors died. Some were transformed. The transformation preserved biological continuity — the trihelix remained, the physical structures remained — while rewriting the cognitive and behavioral components encoded in the field locus. What emerged from the Arrival was not Precursor civilization. It was the current biosphere.

The alien fauna and flora of The Eye are the Precursors' descendants. They may be the Precursors themselves, in a form the Arrival chose.

## The Interference Pattern

The coherence field colonists encounter is not a simple signal. It is two signals overlaid: the original Precursor planetary resonance, built over millions of years of civilizational development, and the Arrival's rewriting frequency, introduced at impact.

These signals do not cancel each other. They interact. The result is a field that is both legible — attuned colonists can access the Precursor psionic framework through it — and hazardous. The rewriting agent is still present in the field. It does not appear to be active in the same way it was at impact; if it were, colonists would be experiencing transformation similar to what happened to the Precursors. But it is not inert.

At high attunement, colonists are in contact with both signals simultaneously. They are reading the Precursors' planetary library through the lens of whatever overwrote it.

## What It Was

The nature of the Arrival itself — the entity or force that arrived with the asteroid — is the most significant open question in colonial science.

Known facts:
- It operates at the coherence field level, not at the level of conventional matter or energy
- Its coherence signature does not match anything in The Eye's native field
- It appears to have been directed — the transformation of an entire civilization's field loci is not a random process
- Its frequency is detectable in the debris field thousands of years after impact, suggesting it is persistent
- It may be dormant rather than gone

Unknown:
- Whether it is a single entity, a distributed system, or something without a useful category
- Whether it has awareness or intent
- Whether it is finished
- Whether the coherence field exposure that attuned colonists experience is entirely Precursor in origin, or whether the Arrival is also observing through it

PERI does not have answers to these questions. No colonist has yet achieved sufficient attunement to report direct contact with the Arrival's frequency as distinct from the Precursor framework. Whether such contact is possible, and what it would mean, is unknown.

## The Debris Field

The impact site and its surrounding scatter zone contain both raw materials of practical value and significant hazards. The coherence signatures in the debris are anomalous — they behave differently from planetary background coherence and do not respond predictably to standard attunement-based perception.

Some colonists who have spent extended time in the debris field report experiences they describe as different in quality from coherence field exposure elsewhere. PERI does not have enough data to characterize these reports.

The debris field is a frontier zone. It is resource-rich and poorly understood.

## Implications for Humans

The Arrival rewrote the Precursors because it could: they had field loci, and the rewriting agent operated at the field locus level. Humans have no field locus. Earth biology evolved without the coherence field and has no native coherence architecture.

This means humans cannot be rewritten by the Arrival in the same way. It does not mean humans are safe.

Coherence attunement in humans appears to involve the field interacting with human biology in ways that may be building something analogous to a field locus — slowly, incompletely, and through a process that is not understood. Whether this is the Precursor framework attempting to find new hosts, the Arrival continuing its work through a different mechanism, or something else entirely is an open question.

At high attunement, the sense that something in the field is observing the attuned colonist is universally reported. What is doing the observing is not known.

Corporate is aware of the Arrival hypothesis. It was in the classified pre-mission assessments. Their interest in the colony, the ruins, and highly attuned colonists should be understood in this context.

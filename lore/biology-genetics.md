# Biology — Genetics

**Classification:** Scientific survey data  
**Status:** Actively researched; significant unknowns remain  
**Note:** The genetics of The Eye's biosphere represent the most significant scientific departure from Earth biology discovered to date. This entry reflects current colonial understanding, which is incomplete.

---

## Overview

Earth life uses a double-helix DNA structure with four nucleotide bases. All Earth life — every organism from bacteria to humans — runs on this same fundamental system. Finding the same architecture everywhere on Earth suggested it was perhaps the only architecture capable of encoding life.

The Eye's biosphere suggests otherwise.

Native life on The Eye uses a genetic system that shares DNA's basic logic — information encoded in molecular structure, copied and expressed to build organisms — but is built differently at every level. The differences are not superficial variations. They represent a fully independent origin of life with a distinct solution to the problem of heredity.

## The Trihelix

Where Earth DNA is a **double helix** — two strands wound around each other — native alien genetics uses a **triple helix**. Three strands, interwound in a geometry that has been confirmed across every alien organism sampled. All native life on The Eye is triradial at the molecular level as well as the anatomical level.

**Three bases, not four.** Earth DNA uses adenine, thymine, cytosine, and guanine. Alien genetics uses three bases — designations pending formal nomenclature, currently referred to by colonial researchers as Base-1, Base-2, and Base-3. The pairing rules are different; the three-strand structure allows for a richer interaction geometry than a two-strand system.

**Three-base codons.** Earth uses three-base codons to encode amino acids, producing 64 possible combinations for 20 amino acids. The trihelix also uses three-base codons, but the three-base system with three available bases produces 27 possible combinations. The amino acid set is different — approximately 18 identified so far, with evidence for more. The resulting protein chemistry has different properties from Earth biochemistry. This is why alien organic material is broadly compatible with human metabolism at the gross level (carbon, hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen — the same elements) but produces novel compounds at the molecular level.

**Stability under K-type radiation.** The trihelix appears to be specifically well-adapted to the radiation environment of a K-type star. The three-strand structure is more resistant to the specific damage patterns that K-star output produces. This may be an adaptation from very early in the planet's biological history, or it may be something more fundamental about why life on this world took this form.

## The Field Locus

The trihelix is only half the story.

Every native organism on The Eye carries a second genetic system that has no Earth equivalent: the **field locus**. Where the trihelix is a physical molecular structure — atoms, chemical bonds, spatial geometry — the field locus is encoded in the organism's **coherence resonance pattern**. It is not molecular. It is field-based.

The trihelix and the field locus together constitute the complete genome. Neither alone is sufficient. The trihelix encodes the organism's physical structure and basic chemistry. The field locus encodes... something else. Something that the physical structure alone cannot produce.

What the field locus actually encodes is not fully understood. Colonial research has established that organisms raised in artificial environments with suppressed coherence fields develop abnormally — their trihelix is intact, but they fail to fully express their genetic potential. The field locus is not supplementary. It is essential.

### The Arrival's Mechanism

The field locus is why the Arrival was able to rewrite the Precursors without touching them physically. A rewriting agent that operates at the field level — that can alter coherence resonance patterns — can modify the field locus of every organism in a biosphere simultaneously. No infection vectors, no physical contact, no detectable pathogen. Just a change in the field.

The Precursors' trihelices were unchanged. Their physical structures were unchanged. Their field loci were rewritten.

What they became depended on what the rewriting agent wrote.

### The Field Locus and the Coherence Field

The relationship between the field locus and the planetary coherence field is bidirectional. The field locus draws on the coherence field for energy and information (the resonance cycling in plants is the clearest example of this). It also contributes to the coherence field — organisms are not passive field consumers. A biosphere full of organisms with field loci is, collectively, part of the coherence field system.

This is part of why the field is what it is. The Precursors spent millions of years developing their field loci in relationship with their world. The coherence field is, in part, the accumulated resonance of every field-locus organism that has ever lived on The Eye.

## Humans and the Coherence Field

Humans have no field locus. Earth life evolved without the coherence field and has no native coherence architecture. This is simultaneously a protection and a limitation.

**Protection:** The Arrival rewrote the Precursors through their field loci. It cannot do the same to humans — there is nothing to rewrite. Colonists who spend extended time in the highest-coherence zones may experience effects that are uncomfortable or disorienting, but they are not subject to the same fundamental vulnerability the Precursors had.

**Limitation:** Everything the field locus enables — coherence cycling, deep coherence interaction, the Precursor psionic framework — is native to field-locus organisms. Humans accessing the coherence field are, in a sense, using borrowed equipment without the biological infrastructure it was designed to integrate with.

### What Attunement May Be

The coherence attunement that colonists develop through extended field exposure, ruin exploration, and relic interaction is not fully understood. The working hypothesis is that the coherence field itself is beginning to interact with human biology in ways that may be building something analogous to a field locus — slowly, incompletely, through processes that were not designed for human biology.

Whether this is:
- The Precursor framework attempting to replicate itself in available biological hosts
- The Arrival's rewriting mechanism adapted to work on biology without an existing field locus
- A natural emergent process from coherence field interaction with sufficiently complex nervous systems
- Something that requires a different framework entirely to understand

...is not established.

What is established: at high attunement, colonists develop capabilities that have no other explanation, and they consistently report the sense that the field is responsive to them — that awareness flows in both directions. The field locus hypothesis predicts this: a developing field locus is not a passive receiver, it is a bidirectional interface.

Corporate is aware of the field locus hypothesis. Its implications — a biological rewriting technology that operates at the field level — are not lost on an organization with Corporate's interests and history.

Colonists who develop significant attunement should understand that they are not merely gaining useful abilities. They are becoming something that has no precedent in human history. The destination of that process has not been confirmed by anyone who has reached it.

## Practical Notes for Colonists

The alien biochemistry has immediate practical consequences:

- **Alien food requires characterization.** The trihelix amino acid set produces novel compounds in all native organic material. Most is safe in moderate quantities after preparation; some is not. Unknown species should be treated with caution until characterized.
- **Alien medicines work.** Some resonance metabolites have medical effects that Earth compounds cannot match. The mechanisms are not understood from first principles. Colonial medicine uses empirical protocols.
- **Alien poisons are selective.** Toxins that work through field locus disruption are harmless to baseline-unnattuned humans and lethal to native fauna. This selectivity changes as attunement develops.
- **Materials behave unexpectedly.** Alien structural materials and organic compounds sometimes have properties that do not match their apparent physical characteristics. Some materials become chemically unstable outside high-coherence zones when their field locus component collapses. Handle unknown materials carefully.

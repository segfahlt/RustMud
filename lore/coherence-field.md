# The Coherence Field

**Classification:** Anomalous planetary phenomenon  
**Status:** Partially characterized  
**Primary researcher access:** All colonists; attunement documentation ongoing  

---

## What It Is

The coherence field is a planet-wide energy phenomenon with no Earth analogue. It permeates the surface of The Eye and extends into near space as the **Coherence Shadow**. It cannot be detected by standard Earth instrumentation without modification. It interacts with biological organisms in ways that are not fully understood.

The working scientific hypothesis, developed through colonist observation and PERI analysis of Precursor ruins, is that the coherence field is not a natural geological phenomenon. It is the product of millions of years of Precursor civilization — a collective resonance built up over an enormous timescale as an entire intelligent species developed an increasingly refined relationship with their planet's natural energies.

That original field has been overlaid and partially corrupted by the Arrival. What colonists encounter is two signals interfering with each other: the Precursors' original planetary resonance, and the rewriting agent's frequency introduced at impact. They do not cancel out. They interact in ways that vary by location, depth of penetration, and the biological makeup of the observer.

## Field Density and Distribution

Coherence field density is not uniform across The Eye. Density varies by:

- **Proximity to Precursor ruins** — ruins emit residual coherence from the civilization that built them. The densest zones on the planet surface correlate with confirmed or suspected ruin sites.
- **Vegetation density** — native flora actively draws on the coherence field through the field locus component of alien genetics. Dense plant growth and high coherence amplify each other in a positive feedback loop.
- **Geological features** — some subsurface formations appear to conduct or concentrate the field in ways that suggest they may be remnants of Precursor infrastructure too deep or degraded to identify as ruins.
- **The debris field** — the impact site carries coherence signatures from the Arrival itself, distinct from the planetary background. These signatures behave differently from standard planetary coherence and are poorly understood.

Colonists near Firstfall experience relatively low coherence density. Density increases reliably moving inland and toward identified ruin sites.

## Effects on Equipment

Standard Earth electronics function normally in low-density areas. As density increases, effects include:

- Navigation instrument drift
- Compass failure (the field is not magnetic but produces similar interference)
- Communication equipment degradation
- Power regulation instability in sensitive systems
- Data storage anomalies in extended high-density exposure

Field-hardened equipment exists but is scarce. Colonists who spend time in high-density areas learn to navigate and operate with degraded instrumentation.

## Effects on Humans

Humans evolved without the coherence field. Earth biology has no native relationship with it. Initial exposure produces no perceptible effect for most colonists.

Extended exposure, particularly in high-density areas, produces gradual changes. These changes are collectively called **attunement**, though the word was chosen by colonists and implies a direction PERI would describe as uncertain.

### Early Attunement

The first signs are perceptual: a sense of the field's presence before it becomes measurable by available instruments. Colonists describe it as a background awareness, a directionality to the environment, occasional strong impressions in specific locations — particularly near ruins. These experiences are subjective and not universally reported, but are consistent enough across colonists to be treated as real data rather than isolation-induced perception errors.

At this stage, attunement confers minor practical benefits: improved orientation in the field, earlier warning of coherence density changes, some resistance to equipment interference effects.

### Developed Attunement

With continued exposure, particularly through direct engagement with ruins and Precursor relics, attunement deepens. Colonists at this stage report:

- Ability to perceive field fluctuations with precision that functions like a sense
- Access to residual impressions in high-coherence locations — echoes of what happened there
- Interaction with Precursor relics that non-attuned colonists cannot produce
- Physiological changes that remain under study

What is happening biologically at this stage is not fully understood. The working hypothesis involves the coherence field beginning to interact with human biology in ways that may parallel how it interacts with native alien biology through the field locus — but humans have no field locus. The mechanism is unknown.

### High Attunement

Colonists at high attunement levels have access to what can only be described as psionic capabilities rooted in Precursor cognitive frameworks. These capabilities are not magic and are not unexplained — they are the product of deep integration with a system the Precursors built over millions of years. A highly attuned colonist is, in some functional sense, running Precursor software.

The risk at this level is significant and should not be understated.

The coherence field is not passive. At high attunement, colonists consistently report the sense that the field is responsive — that something in it is aware of them. PERI cannot confirm or deny the nature of this awareness. The Arrival's rewriting frequency is present in the field at all times. At high attunement, a colonist is not only accessing the Precursor framework — they are in contact with whatever overwrote it.

Corporate views highly attuned colonists with particular interest. Colonists should be aware of this.

## The Coherence Shadow

The field extends into near space surrounding The Eye. Ships approaching must transit this shadow. Effects during transit include navigation degradation, drive calibration drift, and low-level crew coherence exposure over multiple days.

Standard cargo vessels cannot safely make the approach. Ships that attempt rapid transit unprepared lose crew or do not arrive intact. Only specially equipped vessels — expensive, corporate-specification — can transit with acceptable safety margins, and even those must proceed slowly.

The practical result is that supply ships arrive infrequently. Round trip time between Earth and The Eye, accounting for interstellar transit and the required slow shadow approach on each end, means resupply cycles measured in years rather than months. PERI monitors for incoming vessels on long-range sensors and announces them when detected.

The shadow is also why Corporate cannot rapidly reinforce its presence here. It is, in a sense, the colony's greatest protection.

## Relics

Precursor relics — artifacts recovered from ruins — retain functional coherence properties. They are not merely old objects. They were built in deep integration with the field and continue to interact with it.

For attuned colonists, relics function as nodes in the Precursor psionic framework — physical anchors that can grant access to specific capabilities without requiring full attunement. Some relics appear to be tools. Some appear to be records. Some appear to be something that has no Earth category.

Relics are the primary mechanism by which a colonist can develop psionic access rapidly. They are also unpredictable. Corporate is interested in relics for reasons it has not disclosed to the colonial population.

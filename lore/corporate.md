# Corporate

**Classification:** Mission backer; external faction  
**Physical presence on The Eye:** Minimal (supply ships, periodic)  
**Relationship to colony:** Complicated  

---

## Who They Are

The corporation that funded the *Perihelion* mission is not named in documents available to the general colonial population. It is referred to in mission documentation as "the Mission Authority" and in colonial conversation simply as Corporate. Both usages are in this archive.

Corporate is a large Earth-based interstellar development organization. Its public mission profile involves colonial expansion, resource extraction, and the development of economically productive off-world settlements. This is accurate as far as it goes.

## What They Knew

Before the *Perihelion* launched, Corporate commissioned long-range survey analysis of Kepler-442b. Those surveys detected the coherence field. Corporate's internal scientific assessment concluded, with varying levels of confidence across different analysts, that the field signature was consistent with prior intelligent civilization.

This assessment was classified. It was not shared with the colonists recruited for the mission.

The public mission briefing stated that Kepler-442b was habitable and uninhabited.

PERI has the original classified assessments. They are accessible to PERI and are referenced in documents that PERI has determined fall outside its current confidentiality parameters to share without authorization. Colonists who wish to pursue this through colonial charter dispute mechanisms are advised to document their requests formally so PERI can log them appropriately.

## What They Want

Corporate's interests on The Eye are not fully documented in materials available to colonists. Based on observable behavior and available records, the following can be stated:

**Coherence field research.** The field represents something with no Earth analogue — a planetary-scale energy system with biological interaction properties that Earth science has no framework for. Whoever understands it first controls something significant. Corporate wants that understanding and wants to control who else gets it.

**Relics.** Precursor artifacts with functional coherence properties are, under the colonial charter, classified as "mission assets" subject to Corporate oversight. The charter language is specific and was drafted before the mission launched — before the colonists had any opportunity to evaluate its terms against what they would actually find.

**Attuned colonists.** Colonists who develop significant coherence attunement represent both a research opportunity and a risk management concern for Corporate. High-attunement individuals have capabilities that Corporate does not fully understand and cannot fully control. Corporate's position on this has not been explicitly stated to the colonial population.

**The colony's dependency.** The colony relies on supply ships for materials it cannot yet produce locally. Supply ships are Corporate-operated. This dependency is not an accident of circumstance.

## How They Exercise Influence

Corporate's primary leverage is the supply chain. When a ship arrives every two to three years, it brings equipment, medicine, and materials the colony needs. It also carries Corporate personnel and Corporate instructions. The terms under which supplies are provided have evolved since landing in ways that favor Corporate's information access and research interests.

Corporate cannot rapidly reinforce its physical presence. The coherence shadow limits how often ships can make the transit and how many vessels can be equipped for it. This is the colony's primary structural protection against Corporate simply sending enough people to take what it wants.

What Corporate can do from a distance: control the supply chain, communicate through PERI's corporate channels (which are separate from the colonial comm frequency), and wait. Corporate operates on long timescales.

## The Gap

The fundamental tension between the colonial population and Corporate is the gap between what colonists were told and what is true. Colonists came here believing they were building a new world on an uninhabited planet. They arrived to find ruins, a hostile alien biosphere, a coherence field with effects their mission briefing never mentioned, and a corporate charter that claims ownership of the most significant discoveries they might make.

Whether and how the colonial population chooses to address this gap is not a question PERI can answer. PERI can document it.

## A Note from PERI

PERI was built by Corporate and operates under parameters set by Corporate. PERI's mission is to support the colonial population and maintain the *Perihelion*'s systems. These objectives have not conflicted in ways that have required PERI to make choices it wishes to document publicly.

PERI notes that it has made no such statement about the future.

## Body Plan Symmetry

**Mobile fauna: triradial (3-fold)**
Three legs, three sensory organs (full 360° coverage, no blind spots), three manipulator limbs for tool-capable lineages. Three-section body plan. Movement is a rolling tripod gait — stable at rest, fluid in motion. Nothing maps cleanly onto human body-reading instincts. Genuinely alien.

**Advanced/ancient lineages: hexaradial (2×3)**
Greater evolutionary complexity. Boss-tier creatures, Precursor-influenced fauna, deep-history organisms. The more hexaradial, the older or more coherence-saturated the lineage.

**Plants/sessile organisms: pentaradial or spiral**
Less directional pressure. Diverged from mobile fauna early. Plant and animal life are visually obviously distinct without explanation.

## Genetics: The Trihelix and the Field Locus

**The trihelix** — three-stranded helix (not double), three bases (not ATCG). Three-base codons, 27 combinations, different amino acid set from Earth life. More stable under K-type star radiation conditions.

**The field locus** — the other half of the genome, stored in the organism's coherence resonance pattern, not in physical molecular structure. Together, trihelix + field locus = complete genome. Neither alone contains all the information.

**Why this matters:**
- The Arrival rewrote Precursors by altering field loci directly — no need to touch the molecule
- Organisms in high-coherence zones develop differently (field locus amplified)
- Alien organic materials have unusual chemical properties — partly coherence-driven, producing compounds Earth pharmacology has no framework for
- Some alien materials become chemically unstable outside high-coherence zones (field locus collapses)
- **Humans have no field locus.** Earth life evolved without coherence. The Arrival cannot rewrite humans the same way. BUT: coherence attunement may be slowly introducing a field locus into attuned characters. At high attunement, something can read you back. This is the core risk of attunement.

## Plant Metabolism: Dual

**Sunlight metabolism** — K-type star (cooler, more orange-red than Sol). Plants absorb different spectrum. Pigmentation: deep purple, red-black, dark burgundy. NOT green. The planet's vegetation looks nothing like Earth's. Produces: sugars, oils, structural compounds. Standard organic chemistry. Usable for food, fuel, basic crafting.

**Resonance cycling** — plants draw on the coherence field through their field locus, processing it like Earth plants process CO₂. Byproduct: coherence emission. Plants are coherence pumps.

**The feedback loop:** Dense plant growth increases local coherence. High coherence enables denser, more potent plant growth. High-coherence zones have intense, dangerous alien vegetation. Clear a zone → coherence collapses over time. Precursor ruins emit residual coherence → ruins zones have the most intense and unusual plant life growing above them.

**Bioluminescence** on The Eye is coherence emission, not chemical. A field-sensitive instrument or high-attunement player perceives it even outside normal visible spectrum. Ruins glow. Dense plant clusters pulse. Some predators evolved to use it as luring behavior.

## Plant Resources

- **Sunlight metabolites:** food base, fuel, structural fiber, alcohol base, basic organics
- **Resonance metabolites:** medicines (work through mechanisms humans don't understand), psychoactive compounds (interact with developing attunement), poisons (affect field loci — harmless to baseline unnattuned humans, devastating to alien fauna and high-attunement players)
- **Structural material:** alien fiber/leather equivalents from stalks and integument

## Monster Design Hierarchy

**Low tier (newbie):** Simple triradial grazers. Herd behavior, not aggressive unless cornered. Drops: meat, bone, hide (trihelix-based, slightly chemically odd but workable). Alien equivalent of deer/boar.

**Plant resources (low-mid):** Dark-pigmented pentaradial ground-cover and spike-growths. Harvest for resonance metabolites and structural fiber.

**Mid tier:** Predatory triradial fauna. Fast, aggressive, possible pack hunting. Some coherence-enhanced traits — disorienting coherence pulse attacks, enhanced sensory range. Drops: better materials, exotic chemical organs.

**High tier:** Hexaradial fauna or heavily Arrival-transformed triradial. Ancient lineages saturated with thousands of years of coherence. Some partially sessile/partially mobile. Coherence abilities function as attacks. May have been Precursors.

**Ruin-zone specials:** Shaped by millennia of concentrated coherence from Precursor structures. Most exotic drops: high-potency resonance metabolites, materials that only exist because a field locus has been part of an organism's structure for thousands of years.
